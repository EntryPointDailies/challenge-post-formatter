Each modifier has a weight by colour difficulty,
Green: 1
Blue: 2
Purple: 3
Red: 4
To calculate the challenge difficulty, add the weights of each modifier, then divide by 3.

The colour of the embed shall be determined by the category that the number falls into below,
Green: 1.0-1.66
Blue: 2-2.33
Purple: 2.66-3
Red: 3.33-4
